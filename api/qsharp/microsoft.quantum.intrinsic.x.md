Espaço de nome: [Microsoft.Quantum.Intrínseco](xref:Microsoft.Quantum.Intrinsic)

Aplica o portão pauli $X$.

\start{align} \sigma_x \mathrel{:=} \start{bmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ \\ 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}.
\end{align}
